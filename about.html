Katie Marie

I love to see ideas get off the ground. Whether you're a blossoming business, an aspiring artist, or a gregarious group of go-getters, I'm here to help provide a home for you on the web. When not coding, I enjoy spending time outside amongst the trees, exploring new cities and restaurants, live music, reading, learning, knitting, cooking, and spending time with my family. I am a mother to two terrific teenagers, and aunt to numerous nephews (and one nifty niece). I love books and films that make me both laugh and cry.

My workstyle is

Transparent. I update you regularly on the status of the project, so you'll never have unpleasant surprises or hidden costs.

Collaborative. We'll combine your vision with my expertise and work together every step of the way.

Outcome-driven. My goal is to exceed your expectations. We won't stop until you're satisfied with the results.

What to Expect

Building a home on the web isn't all that different than buliding an earthly home. There's the plot of land (hosting), mailing address (domain), blueprints (wireframes), foundation (code, platform, CMS), construction (development, back-end), interior design (website design), and finally, the best part- moving in.

Consultation: We'll have a no pressure chat. We'll identify your needs, your budget, your wish list, your priorities and dreams. Show me what you have so far, and what you need to meet your goals. Do you need a logo? photos? writing? I'll meet you where you are.

Proposal: I'll come back with a selection of designs that fit in your budget, and we'll refine your project goals.

Partnership: This is the time we sign the contract, and a 50% deposit is due. No surprises here, we'll have already agreed on a design and budget.

Development: Clients receive regular updates regarding their project status while I create and test it. This is the time for any minor adjustments. I'm here every step of the way.

Launch: It's so exciting to see your home on the web come to life! The final 50% is due at launch.

Ongoing Support: Peace of mind is delivered with the final project in the form of available ongoing support.
